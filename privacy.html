Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Minamo Poker ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we generate an anonymous user ID. If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from those providers to link your account.

Game Data: We store your game statistics, chip balance, character customization choices, and ranked progression on our servers to provide the game experience.

Crash Reports: We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and performance data to improve app stability. This data is not linked to your identity.

Purchase History: In-app purchase transactions are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of purchases but do not have access to your payment information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or camera
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not display advertisements

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To save your progress and preferences
  • To enable multiplayer gameplay
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To improve app stability through crash reporting

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. Anonymous accounts can be upgraded to linked accounts (Apple/Google) at any time to prevent data loss.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the Settings menu in the app. This permanently removes all your data from our servers including game progress, character customizations, and account information.

Children's Privacy

Minamo Poker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Game Services: For in-app purchases and Sign in with Apple
  • Google Sign-In: For optional account linking
  • Sentry: For anonymous crash reporting
  • Supabase: For backend data storage and authentication

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@minamo.co.
